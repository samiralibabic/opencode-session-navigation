# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a respectful and productive place for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:

- Be respectful and considerate
- Assume good intent when possible
- Give constructive feedback
- Keep discussions focused on the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, or discrimination
- Insults, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate issues, discussions, pull requests, or other project spaces to keep the community healthy.

If you need to report a concern, contact the maintainers through an appropriate private channel where possible. For security-sensitive reports, use the process in `SECURITY.md`.
